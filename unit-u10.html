💬 核心句型 Sentence Patterns

Do you like pizza?
你喜欢披萨吗？
Yes, I do! Pizza is yummy!
是的，我喜欢！披萨很好吃！
Do you like ice cream?
你喜欢冰淇淋吗？
Yes, I do! Ice cream is delicious!
是的，我喜欢！冰淇淋很美味！
Do you like fish?
你喜欢鱼吗？
No, I do not! I do not like fish.
不，我不喜欢！我不喜欢鱼。
What food do you like?
你喜欢什么食物？
I like apples and bananas! They are yummy!
我喜欢苹果和香蕉！它们很好吃！
Do you like milk or juice?
你喜欢牛奶还是果汁？
I like juice! Orange juice is my favorite!
我喜欢果汁！橙汁是我的最爱！

🗣️ 完整对话练习 Full Dialog

🧒 Tom: Do you like pizza?

👧 Amy: Yes, I do! Pizza is yummy! Do you like ice cream?

🧒 Tom: Yes, I do! Ice cream is delicious! Do you like fish?

👧 Amy: No, I do not. I do not like fish. What food do you like?

🧒 Tom: I like apples and bananas! They are healthy!

👧 Amy: Do you like milk or juice?

🧒 Tom: I like milk! Milk is good for me!
