💬 核心句型 Sentence Patterns

Who is this in your photo?
你照片里这是谁？
This is my dad! I love my dad.
这是我爸爸！我爱我的爸爸。
Do you have a sister?
你有姐妹吗？
Yes, I have one sister! Her name is Lily.
是的，我有一个妹妹！她叫莉莉。
Do you have a brother?
你有兄弟吗？
No, I do not have a brother. I am the only child!
没有，我没有哥哥或弟弟。我是独生子！
How many people are in your family?
你家有几口人？
There are four people in my family. Mom, dad, grandma, and me!
我家有四口人。妈妈、爸爸、奶奶和我！
I love my family so much!
我非常爱我的家人！
Me too! My family is the best!
我也是！我的家人是最棒的！

🗣️ 完整对话练习 Full Dialog

🧒 Tom: Who is this in your photo?

👧 Amy: This is my mom and my dad! I love them!

🧒 Tom: Do you have a sister?

👧 Amy: Yes! I have one sister. Her name is Lily. Do you have a brother?

🧒 Tom: No, I do not have a brother. How many people are in your family?

👧 Amy: There are five people. Mom, dad, grandma, my sister, and me!

🧒 Tom: I love my family so much!

👧 Amy: Me too! My family is the best!
