💬 Mini Show 演出句型

Welcome to our show!
欢迎来到我们的表演！
Thank you! We are very happy!
谢谢！我们非常高兴！
Can you sing?
你会唱歌吗？
Yes! I can sing ABC!
会！我会唱ABC！
Can you count to ten?
你会数到十吗？
Yes! One, two, three... ten!
会！一、二、三……十！
You are wonderful! You are a star!
你太棒了！你是一颗星星！
Thank you so much! Let's celebrate!
非常感谢！让我们一起庆祝吧！

🗣️ 完整演出对话 Mini Show Dialog

🧒 Tom: Welcome to our show! My name is Tom.

👧 Amy: My name is Amy. We are happy to see you!

🧒 Tom: I can count! One, two, three, four, five!

👧 Amy: I can sing! A, B, C, D, E, F, G!

👥 All: Thank you for coming! Let's celebrate!

👥 All: Goodbye! See you next time!
