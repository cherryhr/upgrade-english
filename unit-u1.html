💬 核心句型 Sentence Patterns

Hello! I am Amy.
你好！我是艾米。
Hi Amy! I am Tom. Nice to meet you!
嗨艾米！我是汤姆。很高兴认识你！
What is your name?
你叫什么名字？
My name is Tom.
我的名字是汤姆。
Good morning, teacher!
老师早上好！
Good morning! How are you today?
早上好！你今天怎么样？
How are you?
你好吗？
I am fine, thank you! And you?
我很好，谢谢！你呢？
Goodbye! See you tomorrow!
再见！明天见！
Bye bye! See you tomorrow!
拜拜！明天见！

🗣️ 完整对话练习 Full Dialog

🧒 Tom: Good morning! What is your name?

👧 Amy: Good morning! My name is Amy. What is your name?

🧒 Tom: I am Tom. How are you, Amy?

👧 Amy: I am fine, thank you! And you?

🧒 Tom: I am great! See you later!

👧 Amy: Goodbye Tom! See you tomorrow!
