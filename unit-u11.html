💬 核心句型 Sentence Patterns

Do you have a ball?
你有球吗？
Yes, I have a ball!
是的，我有一个球！
I have a teddy bear.
我有一只泰迪熊。
Wow! I like teddy bears too!
哇！我也喜欢泰迪熊！
Can I play with it?
我可以玩它吗？
Yes, you can! Let's play together!
可以！我们一起玩吧！
What is your favorite toy?
你最喜欢的玩具是什么？
My favorite toy is a car!
我最喜欢的玩具是小汽车！
My turn! Your turn!
轮到我了！轮到你了！
OK! I share my toys with you!
好的！我和你分享我的玩具！

🗣️ 完整对话练习 Full Dialog

🧒 Tom: Do you have a ball?

👧 Amy: Yes, I have a ball! Do you want to play?

🧒 Tom: Yes! Can I play with it?

👧 Amy: Yes, you can! Let's play together!

🧒 Tom: Thank you! My turn! Your turn!

👧 Amy: I share my toys with you! We are friends!
