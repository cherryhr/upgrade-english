💬 核心句型 Sentence Patterns

What is this?
这是什么？
It is a cat.
它是一只猫。
Do you like cats?
你喜欢猫吗？
Yes, I do! I like cats very much!
是的，我喜欢！我非常喜欢猫！
Do you like dogs?
你喜欢狗吗？
Yes, I do. Dogs are my favourite!
是的，我喜欢。狗是我最喜欢的！
Do you have a pet?
你有宠物吗？
Yes, I have a rabbit!
是的，我有一只兔子！
What does a cat say?
猫怎么叫？
A cat says meow meow!
猫喵喵叫！
What does a dog say?
狗怎么叫？
A dog says woof woof!
狗汪汪叫！
Which animal do you like?
你喜欢哪种动物？
I like elephants! They are big and strong!
我喜欢大象！它们又大又强壮！
I have a dog. I love my dog!
我有一只狗。我爱我的狗！
Wow! I love dogs too! Can I play with your dog?
哇！我也爱狗！我可以和你的狗玩吗？
