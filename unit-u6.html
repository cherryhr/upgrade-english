💬 核心句型 Sentence Patterns

How many apples do you have?
你有几个苹果？
I have five apples!
我有五个苹果！
How old are you?
你几岁了？
I am six years old!
我六岁了！
Can you count to ten?
你能数到十吗？
Yes! One, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten!
能！一、二、三、四、五、六、七、八、九、十！
How many fingers do you have?
你有几根手指？
I have ten fingers!
我有十根手指！
What number comes after seven?
七的下一个数字是什么？
Eight! Seven, eight, nine, ten!
八！七、八、九、十！

🗣️ 完整对话练习 Full Dialog

🧒 Tom: How old are you?

👧 Amy: I am six years old! How old are you?

🧒 Tom: I am seven years old! Can you count to ten?

👧 Amy: Yes! One, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten!

🧒 Tom: How many apples do you have?

👧 Amy: I have three apples! How many do you have?

🧒 Tom: I have five! Together we have eight apples!
