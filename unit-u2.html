💬 核心句型 Sentence Patterns

Can you sit down, please?
请你坐下好吗？
Yes, I can! I am sitting down.
好的！我坐下了。
Can you stand up?
你能站起来吗？
Yes, I can stand up!
我能站起来！
Please listen to me.
请听我说。
OK! I am listening.
好的！我在听。
Can you raise your hand?
你能举起手吗？
Yes! I raise my hand!
好的！我举起手来了！
Please open your book.
请打开你的书。
OK! I open my book.
好的！我打开书了。
Are you ready?
你准备好了吗？
Yes, I am ready!
是的，我准备好了！

🗣️ 完整对话练习 Full Dialog

👩‍🏫 Teacher: Good morning! Can you sit down, please?

🧒 Student: Yes, I can! I am sitting down.

👩‍🏫 Teacher: Please open your book. Are you ready?

🧒 Student: Yes, I am ready! I open my book.

👩‍🏫 Teacher: Can you raise your hand if you know the answer?

🧒 Student: Yes! I raise my hand! I know!

👩‍🏫 Teacher: Great! Now please close your book. Bye bye!

🧒 Student: Bye bye, teacher!
