💬 核心句型 Sentence Patterns

What color is this?
这是什么颜色？
It is red!
它是红色的！
What color do you like?
你喜欢什么颜色？
I like blue and green!
我喜欢蓝色和绿色！
Do you like yellow?
你喜欢黄色吗？
Yes, I like yellow! It is sunny!
是的，我喜欢黄色！它像阳光一样！
Can you point to the blue one?
你能指出蓝色那个吗？
Yes! This is blue!
好的！这个是蓝色！
What colors are in a rainbow?
彩虹里有什么颜色？
Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple!
红、橙、黄、绿、蓝、紫！

🗣️ 完整对话练习 Full Dialog

🧒 Tom: What color is this?

👧 Amy: It is red! I like red!

🧒 Tom: What color do you like, Amy?

👧 Amy: I like blue and yellow! What color do you like?

🧒 Tom: I like green! Can you see the rainbow?

👧 Amy: Yes! Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple!

🧒 Tom: A rainbow has many colors! Beautiful!
