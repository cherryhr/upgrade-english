💬 核心句型 Sentence Patterns

Can you touch your head?
你能摸摸你的头吗？
Yes! I touch my head!
好的！我摸了摸头！
How many eyes do you have?
你有几只眼睛？
I have two eyes!
我有两只眼睛！
Can you clap your hands?
你能拍拍你的手吗？
Yes! I clap my hands! Clap clap clap!
好的！我拍手了！拍拍拍！
Can you stamp your feet?
你能跺跺脚吗？
Yes! I stamp my feet! Stamp stamp stamp!
好的！我跺脚了！跺跺跺！
Can you wave your arms?
你能挥挥胳膊吗？
Yes! I wave my arms! Hello!
好的！我挥动胳膊！你好！

🗣️ 完整对话练习 Full Dialog

🧒 Tom: Can you touch your nose?

👧 Amy: Yes! I touch my nose! How many ears do you have?

🧒 Tom: I have two ears! Can you clap your hands?

👧 Amy: Yes! I clap my hands! Clap clap clap!

🧒 Tom: Can you stamp your feet?

👧 Amy: Yes! Stamp stamp stamp! Now let's wave our arms together!

🧒 Tom: Yes! I wave my arms! Head, shoulders, knees and toes!
